Pan 40 MG Description

Pan 40 tablet is an acid-reducing medicine. It is used to treat stomach ulcers, gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD) and other acidity-related problems. GERD occurs due to frequent acid reflux (burning pain or discomfort in the chest). This tablet contains pantoprazole as an active ingredient. It acts by reducing the production of stomach acid. It works best when taken on an empty stomach. You should also be watchful for which are the foods and beverages that seem to induce acidity-related problems, try to consume smaller and more frequent meals, have less oily and spicy food, avoid consumption of alcohol and drink plenty of water. For more customised dietary modifications, reach out to your doctor or nutritionist. Pan 40 tablet should be taken as prescribed by the doctor and in doses and duration as prescribed.
