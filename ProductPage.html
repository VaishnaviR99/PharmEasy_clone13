Dolo 650 MG Description

Dolo 650 tablet is a pain-relieving medicine. It also helps reduce fever. Dolo contains paracetamol as its active ingredient. This medicine is used for many conditions such as headache, backache, migraine, toothache, body pain etc. This medicine may be prescribed with other disease conditions such as typhoid or the common cold, to manage fever. Pain is an unpleasant feeling caused by the formation of certain chemicals in the body in response to an injury, tissue damage or disease. Dolo 650 mg tablet works by blocking the formation of these chemicals responsible for fever and pain.
